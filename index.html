The Residents


The first Art Residency project that produces 1/1 digital art with a profit-sharing mechanism

We are a genesis collection of 5555 hand drawn digital artworks representing human emotions. Each resident grants its holder exclusive access to be a part of Art Residencies and shape the future of digital art

Collection

The main utitily of project is Art Residency. There are many digital artists out there who are working solo. We will gather talented digital artists together, create an environment where they can share their experience, inspire each other and create their own artworks. These are the events called Art Residencies. Our Art Residencies will offer a temporary paradise for artists. Our team will bring artists together where they are away from the distractions of everyday life. Attendees will get to meet other passionate, creative artists and spend their days devoted to their work. How does the process work? Holders of our collection will be able to nominate their own or another artist`s artwork for preliminary selection.
